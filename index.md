# Welcome!

Welcome to the website of the Earth-Life Digital Alliance, a union of researchers, data providers and managers that focuses on research data production, organization and use in the domain of interactions between the biosphere and other Earth Systems. The main research focus of the alliance is the better understanding of the role that life plays and has been playing in the Earth System, including their joint history, coevolution, current function and forecasted interactions. 

This synoptic long-term goal can only be reached with an integrated digital representation of data that come from various sources that together form the knowledge basis that can be used for research.

- *Catalouge*: Preliminary Data Resource Catalogue  
- *Hub*: Aggregated resources for finding data about various biological and Earth System data.
